How to play
  • Purrple is looking for a human friend. Help Purrple thrive and grow in your device.
  • Your progress will be saved automatically, come back at any time but don't be long!
  • Purrple's level indicator is on the top left corner of the screen.
  • Purrple's energy indicator has a thunder icon. Generate power and earn points by using the "W" command or by tapping on Purrple. Watch out for draining your resources when generating power!
Last but not least, don't forget to customize the layout with your favorite colors. Good Luck!
