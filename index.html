How to play
  • Hit the alien and the SMASH! button.
  • 💾 Game progress is saved automatically, come back anytime, but don’t stay away too long, resources may drain.
  • Your skill level is displayed in the top-left corner of the screen.
  • Your power is displayed by the ⚡ icon. You gain power by punching the alien, spend power on smashes.
